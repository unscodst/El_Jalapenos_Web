Authentic Mexican Food at Fiesta Jalapeños Mexican Grill

Are you looking for a fun, festive atmosphere? True “south of the border” food? Excellent service? Then join us at Fiesta Jalapeños, a Mexican restaurant known for using only the freshest ingredients  – the only things frozen at Fiesta Jalapeños are the margaritas!

We pride ourselves on our friendly staff, fast service and authentic Mexican food. We make your food only when you order it; if there’s something you want that you don’t see on the menu, just ask.

Bring your friends and family here and experience Fiesta Jalapeños.
