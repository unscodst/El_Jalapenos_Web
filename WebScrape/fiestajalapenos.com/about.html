About Fiesta Jalapeños

A friendly, growing town with a nice atmosphere. That’s what drew us to North Ridgeville.

A love and knowledge of Mexican food, and over 10 years of restaurant experi- ence. That’s what we wanted to showcase to the community.

We’re the Hernandez family, and we ap- preciate the support North Ridgeville and Lorain County have shown to Fiesta Jalapeños, including bringing new friends with every visit, and nominating our Mexican restaurant for the 2007 Lorain County Beautiful Awards. We love food, and, by using only the finest ingredients and providing excellent service, intend to earn your support and patronage, both today and tomorrow.
