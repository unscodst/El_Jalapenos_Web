General Information About Fiesta Jalapeños

Payment Methods

We accept Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express, and Diners Club International credit cards, and, of course, cash.

Takeout

Would you enjoy having Mexican food in your home or workplace, but want the convenience of ready-made? Our Mexican restaurant features all the traditional Mexican dishes, and we can prepare anything on our menu for takeout. Call ahead or stop by to place an order.

Dress

Fiesta Jalapeños promotes a festive and relaxed atmosphere. Dress is casual.

Location

Fiesta Jalapeños Mexican Grill is located at the intersection of Center Ridge Road (U.S. Route 20) and Lear Nagle Road in North Ridgeville, Ohio. There is convenient access to the rest of Lorain County, as well as Cleveland, its western suburbs, Cuyahoga County and Medina County, via Interstate 480, Interstate 90, and the Ohio Turnpike.
