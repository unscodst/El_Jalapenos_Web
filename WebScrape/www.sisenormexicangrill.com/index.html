Authentic Mexican Food at Si Señor
Mexican Restaurants

Si Señor Mexican Restaurants invites you, your family and friends to enjoy authentic Mexican cuisine in a friendly and festive atmosphere in Westlake, Cleveland, and Seven Hills, Ohio. You’ll find the authentic Mexican dishes here that you would expect, along with some unique dishes that can’t be found elsewhere.

We treat our employees like family, and we all take pride in offering exceptional service. Our food is always fresh — we never use frozen ingredients — and we use our own special blend for our Margaritas — which are frozen!

So come celebrate your day with us, and experience Mexican food the way it should be.
