Locations

Si Senor Westlake

Westlake

In Westlake, Ohio, Si Señor Mexican Restaurant is located on Detroit Rd. (State Route 254) just east of Dover Center Rd. There is convenient access to the rest of Cuyahoga County and to Lorain County, as well as Cleveland and its suburbs, via Interstate 90; use the Crocker Rd. exit to the west or the Columbia Rd. (State Route 252) exit to the east.


Kamm's Corners, Cleveland

In Cleveland, Ohio, Si Señor Mexican Restaurant is located on Lorain Ave. just east of Rocky River Dr. (State Route 237) and Kamm's Corners. There is access to the rest of Cuyahoga County and Cleveland via Interstate 90 or Interstate 480. From Interstate 90, use the McKinley Ave. exit South and turn left on Rocky River Dr. Then turn left at Lorain Ave. For access from Interstate 480, exit at West 150th, go north on West 150th, then turn left onto Lorain and Si Senor will be on your right.
