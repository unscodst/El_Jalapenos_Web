About Si Señor

Saludos! Our families have formed a strong bond with northeast Ohio. After working in the restaurant business for over 15 years, we decided to open our first restaurant in 2002.

Today, we’re excited to bring authentic Mexican fare to Westlake, Cleveland and Seven Hills, where we felt we could succeed with our great Mexican cuisine and friendly service. We’ve become known for our special blend of Margaritas and for serving food made with only the freshest ingredients. Our chefs have created unique Mexican dishes that will change your thinking of “standard” Mexican food!

We hope that you will bring your friends and family and stop by to enjoy our food. Once you’ve sampled our fare, we’re confident you’ll come back again and again.
