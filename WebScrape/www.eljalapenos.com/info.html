General Information About El Jalapeños

Payment Methods

El Jalapeños accept Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express, and Diners Club International credit cards, and, of course, cash.

Takeout

Do you want to share authentic Mexican food with friends in your home or colleagues at your workplace? Our takeout menu features all the traditional Mexican dishes. Call ahead or stop by to place an order.

Dress

El Jalapeños promotes a fun, relaxed atmosphere. Dress is casual.

Location

El Jalapeños Authentic Mexican Restaurant is located on West 117th Street between Detroit Avenue and Clifton Blvd. on the border of Lakewood and Cleveland, Ohio. There is convenient access to the rest of Cuyahoga County, as well as Cleveland's western suburbs, via Clifton Blvd. (U.S. Route 6/20, Ohio Route 2), Detroit Avenue, Lorain Avenue, Interstate 71, and Interstate 90.
