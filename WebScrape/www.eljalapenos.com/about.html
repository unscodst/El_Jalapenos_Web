About El Jalapeños

Diversity – that’s what drew us to the Lakewood area. There’s a good mix of young families and single urbanites who take pride in being involved in their community and knowing their neighbors.

A love and knowledge of Mexican food, and a wealth of restaurant experience. That’s what we wanted to share with this community known for its rich mixture of shopping, entertainment and hometown friend- liness.

We’re the Hernandez family, and we appre- ciate the business we have received from the Lakewood and Cleveland communities. We enjoy that our customers take pride in bringing new friends each time they visit. We love food, and, by using only the finest ingredients and providing excellent service, intend to earn your support and continued patronage.
